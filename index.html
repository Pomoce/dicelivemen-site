DiceLiveMen

DiceLiveMen is a creator-facing short-form video automation tool for tabletop RPG and DnD content creators.

Creators can upload or send their own video notes, automatically format them into vertical short videos, add captions, background music and hashtags, preview the result, and publish the final video to their own authorized TikTok account.

Each creator connects their own TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit and explicitly confirms publication before any video is posted.

DiceLiveMen is currently available as a limited beta for creators who want to prepare and publish short-form tabletop RPG content across social platforms.
