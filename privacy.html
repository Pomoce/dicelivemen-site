Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Data Use

DiceLiveMen uses TikTok authorization to publish videos to the authorized user's TikTok account. The application may access basic TikTok profile information required by the TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API.

Video Processing

The application processes videos provided by the owner, including adding intro and outro materials and preparing short-form video output for publication.

Data Sharing

DiceLiveMen does not sell personal data. Video content is shared with TikTok only when the authorized user publishes or uploads content through the application.

Access Revocation

The user can revoke TikTok access at any time through TikTok account settings. After access is revoked, the application will no longer be able to publish to that TikTok account.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the owner of the DiceLiveMen application.
