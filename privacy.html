Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Data Use

DiceLiveMen uses TikTok authorization to allow creators to publish videos to their own authorized TikTok accounts. The application may access basic TikTok profile information required by TikTok Login Kit and the Content Posting API.

Video Processing

DiceLiveMen processes video content provided by creators, including formatting videos for short-form publication, adding captions, background music, neutral visual backgrounds, and other editing elements selected in the workflow.

Publication Control

Creators review their video, title, hashtags, and publishing settings before publication. DiceLiveMen does not publish content to a TikTok account unless the creator has authorized access and confirmed the publishing action.

Data Sharing

DiceLiveMen does not sell personal data. Video content and related publishing information are shared with TikTok only when an authorized creator publishes or uploads content through the application.

Access Revocation

Creators can revoke TikTok access at any time through TikTok account settings. After access is revoked, DiceLiveMen will no longer be able to publish or upload content to that TikTok account.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the DiceLiveMen team at batonometr@gmail.com.
